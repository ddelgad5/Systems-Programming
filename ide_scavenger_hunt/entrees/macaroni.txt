----------------------
CHEF'S SIMPLE MACARONI
----------------------

INGREDIENTS
-----------
- 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- ground black pepper to taste
- 2 cups milk
- 2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded

DIRECTIONS
----------
1. Bring a large pot of water with a light amount of salt to a boil. Add the macaroni, stirring occasionally for about eight minutes until cooked yet still firm. Drain the pasta.
2. Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. Add flour, salt, and pepper then stir until smooth, for about five minutes.
3. Slowly pour milk into the mixture. Stir until the mixture is smooth and bubbling.
4. Add cheddar cheese to mixture and stir until cheese is melted, may take about two to four minutes.
5. Stir macaroni in the cheese sauce until coated.
